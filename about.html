Meet Dr. Brent Patterson, D.C.

Chiropractor & Soft Tissue Injury Specialist

Dr. Brent has a love for the art, science and philosophy of chiropractic, and has been practicing in south Carlsbad since 2008. He is an avid sports enthusiast, with a strong background in treating sports-related injuries. After 10 years of treating injuries as a massage therapist, personal and athletic trainer at SRJC and SDSU, he obtained his Doctorate of Chiropractic in 2004 at SCUHS. He then went on to pursue additional advanced soft tissue and chiropractic training, studying the Graston, Active Release, and Pettibon postural correction techniques.

Dr. Brent's chiropractic and soft tissue goals are to restore homeostatic functions, proper motion and alignment to all joints of the body. His customized treatments will relieve muscles, nerves and fascia of adhesions, tension, and scar tissue, ultimately improving overall function and well-being. Prevention is the best remedy for disease, so eat healthy, think positive, and move with purpose!

Dr. Brent believes that chiropractic treatment is the most sensible and effective way to treat many common health problems. He also believes in the preventative power of chiropractic care. At Evolve, we treat each patient with a comprehensive strategy involving functional testing, soft tissue therapy, chiropractic adjustments, education, nutritional support, and wellness planning.

Dr Brent has personally had the opportunity to work with several NFL football players for the San Diego Chargers, including Takeo Spikes, Ronnie Brown, Eddie Royal. He has also worked with several UFC MMA fighters, including Rogerio Nogueira and Antonio Rodrigo Nogueira (former interim Heavyweight Champion,) Jr. Dos Santos(UFC Heavyweight Champion), Lyoto Machida (Former UFC Light Heavyweight Champion), Demian Maia, Travis Browne, Andre Galvao, Phil Davis, Vinicius Queiroz, Nick Diaz (former Strikeforce Welterweight Champion), Patricky Freire, Patricio Freire, Fabricio Camoes, KJ Noons, and Christiane Cyborg (Strikeforce Featherweight Champion)

In his spare time, Dr. Brent enjoys competing in triathlons, hiking, horseback riding or snowboarding in the Sierras, traveling the globe, and spending time with his wonderful wife Alessandra, their family and friends.
